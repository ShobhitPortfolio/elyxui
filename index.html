About Us

An Established Leader in
Quality Education

ElyxUI International School (EIS) School, Hyderabad is part of the Kytech — one of India's largest school systems with 150 branches across 14 states, serving 85,000 students from LKG to Class XII.

Situated in Girish Park, Kondapur, Hyderabad on 7.05 acres near Hi-Tech City, MVM integrates CBSE excellence with Transcendental Meditation and Maharishi Vedic Science — nurturing complete intelligence in every student.

Leadership

Chairman's
Message

Education is not merely the transfer of knowledge — it is the awakening of the total potential of every child, through the light of Maharishi's Consciousness-Based Education.

At ElyxUI International School, we believe every student carries within them an infinite reservoir of intelligence and creativity. Our mission is to provide an environment — through world-class academics, transcendental meditation, and dedicated teachers — where that potential blossoms naturally.

Kavya Singh Bhardwaj is the most blessed disciple of His Holiness Maharishi Mahesh Yogi Ji. She is Chancellor of Maharishi Mahesh Yogi Vedic Vishwavidyalaya and Principal of ElyxUI International School — guiding 150 schools, 85,000 students across India.
